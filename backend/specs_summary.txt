What Is Serviio? Serviio is an online platform that  connects people with professional  service providers, like plumbers,  electricians, cleaners, or painters, so  they can get home or office  services easily. 2

Why Serviio?  When I moved to Dhaka and  rented a house, I faced many  problems getting basic  services. My electrician wasn’t  available, I needed a plumber  to fix my washroom, and I  wanted to paint some walls. It  was very difficult to find  reliable professionals. That’s  how Serviio came to be.  3

Serviio Unique Features:



1. Live Service Tracking (Safa)



After booking a provider, users can:



Track provider location live

See estimated arrival time

Get status updates:

Accepted

On the way

Working

Completed



Like food delivery tracking, but for home services.





2. Emergency Quick Booking (30-40 mins) (Riya)



A special emergency button for urgent problems:



Electric short circuit

Water leakage

Lock issues

Medical helper



The request becomes high priority and alerts nearby providers instantly.





3. AI-Based Price Estimation (Shoroj)



Before booking, users get an estimated service cost based on:



Service type

Area/location

Time required

Previous market prices



This reduces scams and increases trust.



Example: Fan repair estimated cost: ৳400–৳600





4. Skill Verification Badge and SOS panic button (Oyon)



Providers must pass:



Skill tests

NID verification

Experience checks



Then they receive badges like:



Verified Electrician

Certified AC Technician

Trusted Cleaner



SOS panic button

One tap sends an instant alert to:



Emergency contacts

Serviio support team

Nearby authorities/admin (future scope)



This increases trust and safety.





5. Community Support Network (Facebook Assist System) (Akib)



If no nearby technician is available for a service request, 

Serviio automatically forwards the request to the official Facebook community support page.



Verified service providers from nearby areas can:



View the request

Respond in comments/inbox

Accept the job directly

Contact the customer quickly



This creates a backup support ecosystem beyond the app itself.

Project   Name   : Serviio  Main Idea   :  Beyond   simply   connecting   people   to   service   providers,   the platform   is   designed   as   a   comprehensive,   real-time   management system   with   several   key   functionalities:  ●   Real-Time   &   Location-Based   Discovery:   The   app   uses   live   GPS and   smart   distance   filtering   to   help   users   instantly   locate technicians   who   are   nearby.  ●   Flexible   &   Urgent   Booking   Options:   Users   can   make   instant bookings   or   schedule   future   appointments.   For   urgent breakdowns—like   electrical   sparks,   AC   issues,   or   pipe bursts—there   is   an   "Emergency   Fix"   feature   designed   to provide   service   within   30   to   45   minutes.  ●   Safety   and   Trust   Systems:   The   platform   heavily   emphasizes security   through   an   advanced   verification   system   that includes   ID   and   police   verification   forms,   a   4-digit technician   verification   code,   and   an   SOS   panic   button.   It also   tracks   behavior   and   maintains   service   quality   through detailed   ratings   and   reviews.  ●   Streamlined   Communication   &   Rebooking:   Customers   and providers   can   talk   directly   using   in-app   messaging   and calling.   To   make   future   repairs   easier,   users   can   bookmark their   favorite   technicians   and   use   a   "One-Click   Repeat Booking"   feature   to   hire   them   again   without   searching.  ●   Multi-User   Ecosystem:   The   use   case   diagram   shows   that   the platform   is   built   to   handle   different   types   of users—Customers,   Admins,   Service   Providers,   and   even   Job Seekers   who   can   search   and   apply   for   work—all   while processing   secure   billing   through   a   payment   gateway.

Motivation   :  1.   The   idea   originated   from   personal   challenges   the   creator faced   when   trying   to   get   basic   services   after   moving   to Dhaka   and   renting   a   house.  2.   Specifically,   they   experienced   issues   like   an   unavailable electrician,   needing   a   plumber   to   fix   a   washroom,   and wanting   to   paint   walls.  3.   The   overarching   difficulty   in   finding   reliable professionals   to   handle   these   everyday   problems   directly inspired   the   creation   of   the   Serviio   platform.
