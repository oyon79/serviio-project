Policies and Help

Operational policies for SERVIIO bookings, payments, safety, and support.

Privacy Policy

SERVIIO stores account, booking, payment, support, verification, and location data only for marketplace operations, safety review, and customer/provider support. Uploaded KYC documents are available only to authenticated admins through protected routes.

Terms of Use

Customers must provide accurate booking details and release handshake codes only after the provider arrives. Providers must keep profile, availability, service area, and verification information accurate.

Sales and Refunds

Paid bookings are held in escrow. Refunds for paid bookings require admin or support review, especially when a dispute, safety issue, or service failure is reported.

Support Policy

Customers and providers can open support tickets from their profile dashboards. Refund, dispute, and safety tickets place escrow under review until an admin resolves the case.

Verification

Providers submit NID, police clearance, skill certificates, live selfie, or experience evidence. Admin review decisions are logged for accountability.

Safety

SOS incidents are logged for admin review. Live location sharing and booking-scoped messages help customers and providers coordinate while preserving an auditable record.
